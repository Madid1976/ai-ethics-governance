# AI Ethics and Governance

Resources and discussions on ethical AI development, responsible AI practices, and AI governance frameworks.

## Topics Covered

- **Fairness and Bias**: Understanding and mitigating bias in AI models and datasets.
- **Transparency and Explainability (XAI)**: Techniques for making AI decisions understandable to humans.
- **Privacy and Security**: Protecting sensitive data and ensuring the secure deployment of AI systems.
- **Accountability and Governance**: Establishing frameworks for responsible AI development and deployment.
- **Societal Impact**: Discussing the broader implications of AI on society, employment, and human rights.

## Resources

- **Papers**: Curated list of academic papers and research on AI ethics.
- **Frameworks**: Overview of existing AI ethics guidelines and governance frameworks (e.g., EU AI Act, NIST AI Risk Management Framework).
- **Tools**: Tools and libraries for bias detection, explainability, and privacy-preserving AI.

## Getting Started

1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Madid1976/ai-ethics-governance.git
   cd ai-ethics-governance
   ```
2. Explore the `docs/` directory for detailed resources and discussions.
